ABOUT LOLO CALDOS FARMVILLE RESORT

Lolo Caldo's Farmville Resort is a business that offers a variety of amenities for its guests. The resort is owned by Vivencio M. Calma and was established in March 2015. It is located in Pasumbalas, San Isidro, San Luis, Pampanga. Over the years, the resort has expanded its property and added more amenities, such as a swimming pool, videoke, occasion packages, overnight, accommodations, and activities.

Farmville Resort is a budget-friendly and relaxing vacation destination that is perfect for families, couples, and groups of friends. With its stunning natural beauty and wide range of activities, the resort has become a beloved getaway spot. The Calma family is proud to have created a warm and welcoming environment where guests can experience the best of rural life.

Amenities

We take great pride in ensuring that our guests have a memorable and fulfilling experience at our resort. Our dedicated staff is committed to providing exceptional service and ensuring that every aspect of your stay exceeds your expectations.

Whether you seek relaxation, adventure, or simply a break from the ordinary, our resort amenities are designed to cater to your needs and make your stay truly remarkable. We look forward to welcoming you and providing you with an unforgettable getaway.

Mission

At Lolo Caldo's Farmville Resort, our mission is to provide our guests with a unique and unforgettable experience that highlights the best of our local environment and culture. We strive to offer sustainable and eco-friendly accommodations and activities that showcase the region's agricultural heritage while supporting local farmers and artisans. Our commitment to environmental conservation includes responsible waste management, energy-efficient practices, and the use of renewable resources whenever possible. We also aim to provide a space for spiritual renewal and growth where guests can experience the solemnity, sacredness, and peacefulness of the place. Our hospitable personnel will be trained to assist and give a helping hand to our valued guests, ensuring their stay is comfortable and memorable. We believe that by providing a high-quality, authentic experience that nourishes both the mind and soul, we can inspire our guests to respect and appreciate the natural world and lead a more fulfilling life.

Vission

Our vision at Lolo Caldo's Farmville Resort is to be the premier destination for guests looking for a sustainable and authentic farm-to-table experience where they can also experience the solemnity, sacredness, and peacefulness of the place for spiritual renewal.
